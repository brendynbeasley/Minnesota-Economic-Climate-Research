Minnesota county climate, housing, labor, and migration.

By Brendyn Beasley 2011-2023
Core Finding

Precipitation is the clearest drag

Across the project, wetter county-years line up most consistently with weaker migration, employment, and construction activity.

Research Design

County-year evidence

The dashboard is built from 87 Minnesota counties, 2011-2023 data, and the project’s county fixed-effects results across climate, labor, housing, and income outcomes.

Best Evidence

Permits, firms, and jobs

The cleanest signals come from building permits, construction establishments, employment, and migration. Use Research View to see the formal model results behind them.
